I talk to much hehehe... 😄 😄 😄. Anyway I recently play a open-world underwater adventure survival called Subnautica and honestly I can feel the taste of the sea just like my beautiful beach near my hometown and I just got the most detail description and add a little bit of feeling about the game:

Exploration and Survival:

Underwater World: The game takes place entirely underwater on a diverse and beautifully crafted alien planet.

Resources: Players gather resources from the ocean to craft tools, vehicles, and bases.

Crafting: A vital aspect of the game, allowing players to create tools, equipment, and even underwater bases to survive and explore deeper into the ocean.

Underwater Life:

Fauna and Flora: The ocean is teeming with various forms of marine life, some of which are friendly and others dangerous.

Alien Technology: There are mysterious and ancient alien structures and technology to discover, adding depth to the game's storyline.

Storyline and Progression:

Plot: The game features a compelling storyline involving the exploration of the alien planet, uncovering its secrets, and finding a way to escape.

PDA (Personal Digital Assistant): Players receive guidance and information through a device called the PDA, which helps in understanding the game world and its inhabitants.

Base Building:

Habitat Building: Players can construct underwater bases by building compartments, adding power sources, and integrating various modules for different functionalities.

Customization: Bases can be customized in terms of layout, decoration, and functionality, providing a sense of ownership in the alien world.

Survival Challenges:

Threats: Players face various challenges, including aggressive creatures, environmental hazards, and the constant need for sustenance.

Depth and Pressure: As players dive deeper into the ocean, they encounter increased pressure and darkness, making exploration more challenging and rewarding.

Expansion and Sequel:

Subnautica: Below Zero: This is a standalone expansion to the original game, featuring a new storyline, biomes, and gameplay mechanics. It explores the icy regions of the same alien planet.

Subnautica received widespread acclaim for its immersive gameplay, stunning graphics, and intriguing storyline. Players praised its unique take on the survival genre and the sense of wonder it instills as they explore the depths of an alien ocean.
